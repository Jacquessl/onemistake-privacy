Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18.12.2025

This Privacy Policy describes how OneMistake ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Analytics Data

The App collects anonymous analytics data to understand how users interact with the game and to improve gameplay and performance.

  • Randomly generated anonymous user identifier (UUID)
  • App interactions (game progress, levels reached, session duration)
  • Approximate location (country or region level only, derived from IP)
  • Technical diagnostics (crash reports, performance data)

Analytics are collected using PostHog.

b) Advertising Data

The App displays third-party advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

  • Ads are non-personalized
  • Ads are shown only after a game over screen
  • No tracking across apps or websites

2. How We Use Information

  • Analytics and gameplay improvement
  • App stability and performance
  • Displaying non-personalized advertisements

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data.

Data may be processed by trusted third-party providers:

  • PostHog (analytics)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)

4. Data Retention

Analytics and diagnostic data are retained only as long as necessary to improve the App and ensure stability.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.
